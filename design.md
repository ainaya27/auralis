# Auralis Design System

## Product Vision

Auralis is an offline-first local music player designed for users who want a clean and distraction-free listening experience.

No streaming.
No account.
No advertisements.
Just your music.

---

# Brand Personality

- Calm
- Modern
- Personal
- Warm
- Nostalgic

Auralis should feel like listening to music during sunset.
